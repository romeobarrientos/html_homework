Restaurant Favorites

Romeo's Favorite Restaurants!

This list only includes restaurants in the Phoenix Metro Area.
Click any photo to view more information about each restaurant.

  1. Roka Akor

    2. Located in Scottsdale, Arizona. This restaurant charms everyone before even getting inside of the building. With two large fire displays creating a warm, welcoming entrance into the building. All the food is prepared on arrival the same day as it is being served. They have a selection of Wagyu steaks that, while pricey, are worth trying once.

  2. Doughbird

    3. This restaurant sports several locations in Arizona. However, the one in Phoenix is great. With an airy, indoor ambience as well as a great outdoor patio. The chicken is to die for at this restaurant. Also, you can not go wrong with any of the appetizers or sides. All this for a very reasonable price point!

  3. Nishikawa Ramen

    4. While this restaurant only has a few Ramen choices to choose from, each of them brings their own strengths to the table. With a huge assortment of add-ons you also have the capability to make your Ramen unique to your preferences. The price point is great here and I wish I could eat here more often!

  4. Harumi Sushi

    5. Famous for its purple sushi, this restaurant always tastes fresh and feels comforting. The staff is very friendly and it also doubles as a great date spot to impress anyone you know! The only pet peeve I have with this restaurant is the parking situation. But, that can be said about any restaurant located in downtown Phoenix.

  5. My Kitchen
